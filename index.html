About us

HollanderSMP is a crazy Minecraft Survival server with season 2 on the latest Minecraft version. Our 24/7 online server has a friendly community and an active team of moderators and admins. Hacks are not allowed to keep the gameplay fair. Come build and play in our public SMP experience and see for yourself what HollanderSMP has to offer!

On HollanderSMP you can now claim your own land to protect your structures from other players. Join our friendly community on our public Minecraft Survival server and enjoy this new feature and more. Come along and claim your own land today!
